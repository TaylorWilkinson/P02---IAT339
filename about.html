Welcome to Sprout:

  • We keep things simple
  • We care about your needs, not just your product portfolio
  • We're a credit union, meaning you're more than just a number

But, what is a credit union?

  • Being a credit union means that we are a member owned and operated.
  • That means that everything we do has our members and our community in mind.
  • Each member owns a share in Sprout, so every member has an equal say in our future.

How do we help you?

  • We provide our members with a comprehensive banking experience, both in person and online, for the modern customer.
  • We help you save money.
  • We help ensure your money is working for you.
  • Our staff and advisors provide you with peace of mind about your finances.
